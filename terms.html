7. Cancellations and refunds

Changing your mind versus slaughter timings

Because fresh meat involves planned slaughter and limited inventory, last‑minute cancellations have real consequences at the farm level.

As a general guide:

Any refunds, once approved, will be processed to your original payment method or another agreed channel within a reasonable time, subject to bank or gateway processing.
