Section 01

Overview

At Farm Mutton Telangana, we follow a pre-order only, fresh-slaughter model. Orders are accepted until Sunday 9 AM, and your mutton is slaughtered fresh that same Sunday morning and delivered within 2 hours. Because of the perishable and made-to-order nature of our products, our refund and cancellation policy reflects this reality.

Please read this policy carefully before placing an order. By placing an order with Farm Mutton Telangana, you agree to the terms outlined below.

🌿
Our commitment to you: We take full responsibility for the quality of every order. If the issue is on our side — wrong cut, wrong weight, spoilage, or non-delivery — you will always receive a full refund or free replacement, no questions asked.
Section 02

Cancellation Policy

2.1Free Cancellation Window

You may cancel your order free of charge at any time before Saturday 8:00 PM of the same delivery week. To cancel, contact us via:

    >📱 WhatsApp: +91-9494051840 >✉️ Email: hello@farmmutton.com

2.2Late Cancellations — After Saturday 8 PM

Cancellations requested after Saturday 8:00 PM cannot be accepted. By this time, the goats have already been allocated against your confirmed order and the slaughter and processing schedule is locked. No refund will be issued for late cancellations.

⚠️
Important: Saturday 8:00 PM is a hard cutoff. Please plan accordingly and cancel well in advance if needed.

2.3No-Show / Unavailability at Delivery Address

If you are unavailable at the delivery address and do not respond to our delivery agent's calls or WhatsApp messages within 15 minutes of arrival, the order will be marked as delivered and no refund will be issued.

Please ensure someone is present to receive the order on Sunday morning. If you anticipate being unavailable, contact us on WhatsApp before Saturday 8 PM to reschedule or cancel.

Section 03

Refund Policy

3.1We Do Not Accept Returns

Because all our products are fresh, perishable meat slaughtered and delivered on the same morning, we do not accept physical returns of any product once it has left our facility. This is in line with FSSAI guidelines for perishable food products.

3.2Quality Guarantee — Eligible for Refund or Replacement

We take full responsibility for the quality of every order. You are eligible for a full refund or free replacement (at our discretion) in the following cases:

Situation Eligibility
Wrong cut or wrong weight delivered Full refund or replacement
Meat has a foul odour or visible spoilage at the time of delivery Full refund or replacement
Order not delivered despite confirmed pre-order Full refund
Incorrect product (e.g. keema delivered instead of curry cut) Full refund or replacement
Quality clearly does not match organic / grass-fed standard as described Partial or full refund after review

3.3How to Raise a Quality Complaint

To be eligible for a refund or replacement, follow these steps:

    > 1
    Contact us within 2 hours of delivery via WhatsApp at +91-9494051840. Complaints raised after 2 hours will not be eligible.
    > 2
    Share clear photos or a short video of the issue — packaging, product condition, weight on scale if applicable.
    > 3
    Our team will review and respond within 4 hours on the same Sunday.
    > 4
    Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method or via UPI / bank transfer.
💬
Tip: WhatsApp is the fastest channel. Send your order details, photos, and a brief description of the issue in one message for the quickest resolution.

3.4Situations Not Eligible for Refund

The following are not eligible for refunds:

    >Change of mind after the Saturday 8 PM cutoff. >Dissatisfaction with taste, spice level, or cooking results — these are subjective and unrelated to product quality. >Improper storage by the customer after delivery (e.g. leaving meat unrefrigerated for several hours). >Partial use of the product before raising a complaint. >Claims made more than 2 hours after delivery.
Section 04

Whole Goat Orders

Whole goat orders (starting from ₹9,000) require a minimum 50% advance payment at the time of booking. The following cancellation terms apply:

Before Friday 6:00 PM Full advance refunded — no deductions, no questions asked.
Friday 6:00 PM – Saturday 8:00 PM 25% of the advance will be retained as a processing and allocation fee. The remaining 75% will be refunded within 3–5 business days.
After Saturday 8:00 PM No refund on the advance. The goat has been allocated and the slaughter schedule is locked.
📞
For whole goat orders, final pricing is based on actual weight. Please confirm the final price via WhatsApp at +91-9494051840 before making the advance payment.
Section 05

Prepaid Orders & Payment

Currently, most orders are confirmed via WhatsApp and payment is collected on delivery or via UPI at the time of order confirmation. For prepaid UPI orders, the following terms apply:

5.1Refund Method

Approved refunds are returned to the same UPI ID or bank account used for the original payment. We do not issue refunds via cash or alternate payment channels unless agreed upon in writing.

5.2Refund Timeline

Refund processing time is 3–5 business days after approval. In most cases, UPI refunds are received within 1–2 business days.

5.3No Processing Fees

We do not charge any processing fee on approved refunds. The full approved amount is returned to you.

Section 07

Modifications to an Order

Order modifications — including change of cut, quantity increase or decrease, or delivery address — are accepted until Saturday 8:00 PM. After that time, no modifications can be made as the allocation is finalised.

7.1How to Modify

Contact us on WhatsApp at +91-9494051840 with your order details and the changes you need. Our team will confirm the modification and any price difference via WhatsApp.

⏰
Saturday 8:00 PM is the last time to modify or cancel. We recommend contacting us by Saturday 6:00 PM to allow our team enough time to process your change.
Section 08

Free Delivery & Minimum Order

8.1Free Delivery Threshold

Free delivery applies to all orders above ₹500 delivered to our covered West Hyderabad locations: Gachibowli, Kondapur, Manikonda, Narsingi, Kokapet, Financial District, Tellapur, Serilingampally, Miyapur, and surrounding areas.

8.2Delivery Charge Refunds

Delivery charges (if applicable on smaller orders) are non-refundable unless the non-delivery or issue was entirely our fault. In cases where we failed to deliver, the full order amount including any delivery charge will be refunded.
